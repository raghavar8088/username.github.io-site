📋 Recommended Investment Strategy — March 2026

Continue SIPs in large-cap and flexi-cap funds; do NOT pause them during volatility.
Avoid fresh lump-sum in midcap/smallcap funds at current P/E of 32×+; wait for a 15–20% correction.
Banking & Financials sector offers best risk-reward — P/B below historical average with strong loan growth.
FMCG and Pharma as defensive allocation (15–20%) if risk appetite is low.
IT sector: Cautious. US recession fears and AI disruption weigh on near-term revenue guidance.
Infrastructure / Capital Goods: Selective buys on dips; government capex cycle remains multi-year.
Keep 10–15% in liquid/short-term debt funds as dry powder to deploy on sharp market corrections.
Auto & EV ecosystem: Interesting for 3–5 year horizon but near-term demand moderation expected.
